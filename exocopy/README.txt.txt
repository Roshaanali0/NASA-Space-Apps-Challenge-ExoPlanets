This folder contains all the required files for the frontend of the website to operate it includes functionality like a navbar and video background on the landing page. When you scroll down you will be greeted by three cards that give you simple information about exoplanets. Below that is a searchbar that lets you search for exoplanets and also you can scroll left and right through them. This part gives you information about each exoplanet what its attributes are and if it is habitable or not. Then below that is a gemini integrated chatbot that can answer your questions related to chatbots and lastly there is a slide in the form of a carousel at the bottom of the website that gives you an overview of what th project is.
